Write _to-day_, _to-night_, _to-morrow_ (but not _together_) with hyphen.
{: lang="en"}

_to-day_, _to-night_, _to-morrow_はハイフンを付けて書くこと（ただし_together_は除く）。
{: lang="ja"}

Write _any one_, _every one_, _some one_, _some time_ (except the sense of _formerly_) as two words.
{: lang="en"}

_any one_, _every one_, _some one_, _some time_は2語に分けて書くこと（ただし_formerly_（以前は）という意味の場合を除く）。
